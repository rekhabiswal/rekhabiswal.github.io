Research

I am interested in the representation theory of infinite-dimensional Lie algebras, especially its intersection with algebraic combinatorics and number theory (modular forms, mock-theta functions).

  2. R. Biswal, V. Chari, L. Schneider, S. Viswanath; Demazure Flags, Chebyshev Polynomials, Partial and Mock Theta functions, Journal of Combinatorial Theory, Series A, 140 , (2016), pages 38-75. [arXiv:1502.05322]
