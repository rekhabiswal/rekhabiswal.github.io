About me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at University of Edinburgh, UK. Prior to this, I was a postdoc at Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn(2018-20) and Université Laval, Québec City(2016-18). I completed my PhD in 2016 under the supervision of Sankaran Viswanath at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences (IMSc), Chennai.

You can have a look at my CV here. In this webpage, you can also find information about my research, and academic activities (organizational activities, conferences, talks).
